The Beginning

From Jordan
to Raleigh

Chef Ayman brought recipes passed down through generations of his family. The flavors of Jordan and Palestine found a new home on Hillsborough Street, right outside Shahrazad Coffee. With 35 years in the food industry, his craft shows up in every plate.

The truck has been rolling for 8 years now, and it quickly became a late-night institution. Word spread through the NC State campus and beyond: the shawarma is the real deal, and the kunafa is unlike anything else in the state.

Family First

A true
family business

This isn't a franchise or a chain. Chef Ayman runs the grill. His daughters help in the kitchen. Every customer gets treated like a guest in their home. That personal touch shows in every bite and every interaction.

From intimate dinners to catering celebrations of over 1,200 people, the family scales with the same care. The food never changes. The love never wavers.
