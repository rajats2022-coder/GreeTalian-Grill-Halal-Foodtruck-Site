Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GreeTalian Grill halal?

Yes — GreeTalian Grill is 100% halal certified. Every item on the menu uses halal-sourced beef, lamb, and chicken, prepared according to Islamic dietary guidelines. It's one of Raleigh's most trusted halal-certified food trucks.

Where is GreeTalian Grill located near NC State University?

GreeTalian Grill is parked at 3823 Hillsborough St, Raleigh, NC 27607 (outside Shahrazad Coffee) — less than 1 mile from NC State University's main campus. It's the closest halal food truck to NCSU, easily walkable or a quick drive from campus. Free parking is available in the lot and on the street.

What are the hours?

GreeTalian Grill is open 7 nights a week starting at 6:00 PM — Monday through Sunday. They stay open late to serve the Raleigh night crowd. Note: kunafa often sells out, so calling ahead is recommended: (919) 744-8619.

What food does GreeTalian Grill serve?

The menu features halal shawarma wraps (beef, chicken, or beef & lamb kooftah at $8 each), a falafel wrap ($7, vegetarian), and authentic Nabulsi-style kunafa in personal (6″ $10), family (10″ $25), and party (15″ $40) sizes. The kunafa is made with stretchy Nabulsi cheese, rose-water syrup, and crushed pistachios — available in fine or coarse texture.

Does GreeTalian Grill offer catering?

Yes — GreeTalian Grill caters events of all sizes, from small private gatherings to large-scale events with 1,200+ guests. Chef Ayman creates custom menus with shawarma stations, kunafa, and full Middle Eastern spreads. To request a quote, call (919) 744-8619.
